Important

Important Notes

Please read before registering to avoid schedule conflicts and missed deadlines.

No Refunds

No refund/credit for any contest cancellation. Please always check with your home school first.

Perfect Score Waiver

TTMath students only: Contest fee is waived for the corresponding contest if you had a Perfect score with TTMath last school year.

Limited Spots

First come first service. Each contest has limited spots and will close before the deadline without notification if spots are gone.

One Registration Rule

For any contest (in a box in the first column) you can only register once in a school year—if you are taking it at your day school, you cannot take it again at TTMath.
