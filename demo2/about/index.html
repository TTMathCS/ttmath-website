About TTMath School

From competition math to top universities since 2010

Our Mission

TTMath School is dedicated to nurturing mathematical and computational talent. We guide students from foundational learning through to international competition success, helping them develop problem-solving skills that last a lifetime.

Our Story

Founded in Markham, Ontario, TTMath has grown from a small tutoring group into one of Canada's leading mathematics and computer science training institutions. Our students have represented Canada at the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO), European Girls' Mathematical Olympiad (EGMO), and Canadian Computing Olympiad (CCO).

Our Philosophy

We believe in building deep understanding rather than memorizing formulas. Our structured curriculum takes students through progressive levels of difficulty, from Funmath for elementary students to olympiad-level training. We combine rigorous practice with creative problem-solving techniques developed through years of competition coaching.

Our Approach

Expert Instructors

Learn from coaches who have guided students to IMO and EGMO medals. Our instructors include former olympiad participants and experienced competition coaches.

Proven Curriculum

Progressive levels (L1→L2→L3→L4) with clear targets. Each level prepares students for specific competitions while building toward higher goals.

Small Class Sizes

Personal attention ensures every student gets the support they need. We identify weak areas and provide targeted practice.

Results-Focused

We track progress through regular assessments and competition results. Our students consistently achieve top rankings nationally and internationally.
